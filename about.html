About Us

Our Mission

We are dedicated to helping children and families recover from
child-abuse related trauma by providing every resources possible
under one roof. We want to make Houston a safer community and end
the cycle of child abuse.

Our Services

Here at the Safe Children Alliance center we offer counseling and
support for victims and their families. We are also partnered with
hospitals across Houston to aid our clients with medical and
mental health issues, as well as agencies that coordinate
investigations into child abuse cases.
